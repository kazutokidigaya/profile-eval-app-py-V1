How to Use this Tool to Evaluate Your Profile
and Plan Your Next Steps

Step 1: Fill Out a Brief Form

Start your journey by providing some basic information about yourself and your career aspirations. This will help us tailor the evaluation to your unique profile

Step 2: Upload Your Resume/CV

Attach your most recent resume or CV. Our tool uses this information to assess your academic background, professional experience, and extracurricular activities.

Step 3: Wait for Our AI to Do Its Magic

Sit back and relax while our advanced AI analyzes your details. It cross references your profile with management program criteria to provide a comprehensive evaluation.

Step 4: View Your Analysis

Receive a detailed report on your fitment for management programs. Understand your strengths, areas for improvement, and how you compare to typical program candidates

Step 5: Schedule a Call with an Expert

Take it further by scheduling a consultation with one of our experts. They will help you interpret your results and discuss your next steps towards management success.
